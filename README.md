# Imperial Ambitions Wiki

Independent English guide hub for **Imperial Ambitions** at
`https://www.imperialambitions.wiki`.

The launch site covers:

- a stable first-campaign plan;
- production, labor, food, resources, trade, towns and logistics;
- seven agent roles, social classes, traits, disease and unrest;
- exploration, colonization, factions, maps, research and diplomacy;
- regiments, battles, ships and the current disband-unit status;
- official Windows/macOS requirements, a no-score review and safe Steam links;
- Article, BreadcrumbList, ItemList, WebSite, FAQPage, VideoGame and VideoObject schema.

## Source policy

- Current Steam release facts and developer answers outrank old demo behavior.
- The Steam-linked manual is used as mechanic documentation, with dates and
  live tooltips taking priority for version-sensitive details.
- Community performance claims remain labeled unless officially confirmed.
- No fake codes, unsupported tier lists, cracks, APK mirrors, trainers or
  extracted game data.

Imperial Ambitions and official materials belong to their respective rights
holders.
